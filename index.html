Who We Are

The Quantitative Research in Economics and Financial Modeling (QREFM Lab) is a specialized unit in the research and application of mathematical tools to build economic and financial models. We are dedicated to fostering innovation and guiding the next generation of researchers.

Welcome to QREFM Lab

Shaping the future of quantitative research.

Our lab is committed to excellence. We strive to build a professional research environment, conduct cutting-edge academic research, and provide comprehensive guidance to students in implementing theoretical and applied research projects.

In addition, we also support students in pursuing postgraduate scholarships, providing them with the necessary resources and guidance to achieve their full potential.
