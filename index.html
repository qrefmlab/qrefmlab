Who We Are

The Quantitative Resources for Economic and Financial Modeling (QREFM Lab) is a specialized unit in the research and application of mathematical tools to build economic and financial models.

Objectives and Mission

  • Build an internationally oriented research lab.
  • Conduct academic research in Mathematical Economics and Mathematical Finance: reading scientific papers, attending and writing for conferences and academic journals related to the field.
  • Guide students/trainees in scientific research, implementing theoretical and applied research projects.
  • Support students/trainees in participating in prestigious international competitions and applying for scholarships abroad.

Research Directions

  • Research on quantitative financial models (pricing, hedging, trading).
  • Research on mathematical models of market microstructure (market liquidity, mean-field games, LOBs, etc.).
  • Research on modern decision-making models and game theory.
  • Research on macroeconomic models incorporating climate change, climate risk, and uncertainty.
  • Application of machine learning tools to solve complex economic and financial models.
  • Combining mathematical tools and economic theories to study problems in economics and business, focusing on market efficiency, collective decision-making, as well as pricing policies, advertising, and optimal contract design for enterprises.
